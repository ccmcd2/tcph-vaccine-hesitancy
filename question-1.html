Frequently Asked Questions

How do we know that the COVID-19 vaccines are safe?

Millions of people have safely received a COVID-19 vaccine.

Over 442 million doses of COVID-19 vaccine have been given in the United States from December 14, 2020, through November 15, 2021.

COVID-19 vaccines are safe and effective. COVID-19 vaccines were evaluated in tens of thousands of participants in clinical trials. The vaccines met the Food and Drug Administration’s (FDA) rigorous scientific standards for safety, effectiveness, and manufacturing quality needed to support approval or authorization of a vaccine.

Millions of people in the United States have received COVID-19 vaccines since they were authorized for emergency use by FDA. These vaccines have undergone and will continue to undergo the most intensive safety monitoring in U.S. history.
